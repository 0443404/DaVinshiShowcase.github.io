Hello there fellow adventurer!

There are many adventurers waiting for you!
Let's go on a journey to see what kind of adventurers await us!


Fps Modified

Boom! Hear that? That was from the galactic space weapon that you have in your hand! In this game you can shoot enemies with that very same weapon, earn other weapons that has special bullets and get white pluses to help you fight strong enemies that will come your way!


Ruby's New Adventure - Time to power up

Oh no! There's something wrong with the robot's systems and now they are wrecking havoc in the city and the forest! Help ruby fix the robots back to their original selves and fix the rampaging robot that is disturbing the other town!


If You give a Pest a Veggie

Everything is going fine at your farm, feeding the cute little animals when...BAM! Comes an invasive monster that threatens to eat your farm! Hurry, grab your tools to defeat the monster before the other farmers find out!


Shadows of the Tree

Today is your big day! You have been chosen as the village protector! As you get ready to do your duties, you think about the shadows that keeps showing up and get ready for what's about to come about these "shadows".


Pirate Chess

There's a rumor going around that there's an enourmous treasure at the bottom of the sea but...arg! News spread fast and now there are other pirates ready to take that treasure away from you! Hurry and race the other pirates before they get to it first!
